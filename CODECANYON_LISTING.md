## Full Description:

### Why MediBot?

MediBot is not just another chatbot - it is an enterprise-grade AI medical assistant built with FAANG-level architecture. While other medical chatbots use a single AI model and support only English, MediBot uses 6 AI models with intelligent routing, supports 31+ languages including Hindi, Bengali, and Arabic, and features voice input/output, patient memory, and a dedicated medical study mode.

### What Makes MediBot Different?

Most AI chatbots on the market are simple wrappers around ChatGPT. MediBot is architecturally different:

- 3-Path Intelligent Routing: Simple greetings take the Instant Path (under 1 second), symptoms take the Fast Path, and complex medical queries take the Slow Path with full WHO guideline integration
- 6 AI Models: Each query is routed to the best model based on intent, complexity, and language
- Conversation Engine: Detects if the user is scared, urgent, confused, or calm and adapts the response tone accordingly
- Response Validator: Every response is validated for quality and completeness before sending. Low-quality responses are automatically retried with a stronger model
- Feedback Learning: The system learns from thumbs up and down ratings to improve future responses
